Steps to Secure Your Social Media

1. Strong Passwords

Create unique and complex passwords for each account and update them regularly.

2. Two-Factor Authentication

Enable 2FA to add an extra layer of security for your social media accounts.

3. Privacy Settings

Configure privacy settings to control who sees your posts, profile, and activity.

4. Recognize Phishing

Be aware of suspicious links and messages to prevent unauthorized access.

5. Regular Monitoring

Check account activity regularly and remove unknown devices or apps.

6. Backup & Recovery

Keep account recovery options updated and maintain backups for important data.
